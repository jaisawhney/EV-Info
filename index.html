Why Electric?

Many say that electric cars are the future of transportation. With gas-powered cars contributing greatly to global warming, electric cars have become a popular alternative. Electric cars have zero emissions and have little maintenance when compared to their gas-powered counterparts. Furthermore, advancements with EV technologies have narrowed the gap between EVs and gas-powered cars.
